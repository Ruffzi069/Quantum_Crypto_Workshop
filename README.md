
# Quantum Cryptography for Hackers

This documentation is not a research paper.

It’s written the way a senior explains things to juniors
after a CTF — casually, with intuition and real-world context.

## What you’ll learn

- What quantum computers actually are
- Why they are a threat to classical cryptography
- How RSA breaks (classical vs quantum)
- What *Harvest Now, Decrypt Later* means
- How Quantum Cryptography works
- QKD and BB84 (with live demo)
- Why Post-Quantum Cryptography matters
